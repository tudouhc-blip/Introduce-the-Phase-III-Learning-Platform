Introduce the Learning Platform
How does the platform embed in the EdUHK–SJTU project?
a · Context
Part of the Framework Toolkit
The Learning Platform is one component of the toolkit for the GenAI-Responsive Competency Framework for University Teachers, developed by professors at EdUHK & SJTU (incl. Dr Dai & Chirp). The Framework is the core of the project — all deliverables (self-assessment tool, learning platform, guidebook, etc.) sit under its umbrella.
